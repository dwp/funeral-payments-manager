Eligibility for a Funeral Expenses Payment

The claimant would not be eligible for payment if the:

  • award has already been made for deceased
  • claimant has not provided sufficient evidence
  • claimant does not receive qualifying benefits
  • funeral was not in the UK, EEA or Switzerland
  • deceased did not usually live in the UK
  • funeral was more than 6 months ago
  • claimant is not responsible for paying for the funeral
